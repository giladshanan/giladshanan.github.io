Soul Dumpling:
Heart Warming Comfort Food Recipes from Around the World

Image by: Pincinc

Comfort food is good for the soul, and cultures from every corner of the globe have developed their own unique ways of elevating the spirit through the culinary arts. I have collected a few of my favorite recipes here for you to enjoy.

I love dumplings (maybe you guessed that already?) but this site includes many non-dumpling related recipes. For a more focused exploration of the delightful world of dumpling-dom, please visit the friendly folks at The Dumpling: A Seasonal Guide.
